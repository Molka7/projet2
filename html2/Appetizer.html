Appetizers

  • Brik
    A crispy deep-fried pastry filled with a perfectly runny egg, tuna, parsley, and spices. A popular Tunisian starter with a delightful crunch and savory flavor.

  • Mechouia Salad
    A smoky roasted vegetable salad made with grilled peppers, tomatoes, onions, and garlic, drizzled with olive oil and garnished with boiled eggs and tuna. A refreshing and flavorful beginning to your meal.

  • Harissa Dip with Bread
    A traditional spicy chili paste served with warm bread. Often paired with olives and preserved lemons for a bold and zesty taste.
