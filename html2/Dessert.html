Desserts

  • Makroudh
    A semolina pastry filled with dates or almonds, fried to golden perfection, and drizzled with honey. A sweet and satisfying treat.

  • Assidat Zgougou
    A quintessential Tunisian dessert made from pine nut paste blended into a rich, smooth pudding, layered with sweet cream, and topped with crushed nuts and sprinkles. Often served during special occasions, this dessert is a perfect blend of tradition and indulgence.

  • Baklawa
    Layers of phyllo dough filled with nuts, spiced with cinnamon, and soaked in orange blossom syrup. A luxurious dessert perfect for ending the meal.
